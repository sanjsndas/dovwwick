Our roots · a chronicle

1986Dovewick founded as a private estate garden, focusing on heritage fruit trees.
1994First public open day; the library of garden manuscripts begins.
2003Established the Verdure House trust, dedicated to ecological horticulture.
2012Launched the “Soil & Stem” journal — now a quarterly print publication.
2020Expanded into digital archives, making our knowledge accessible worldwide.
2026Celebrating 40 years of quiet cultivation. New woodland garden underway.

Keepers & cultivators

Our team is small, deliberate, and deeply rooted. We are gardeners, writers, and ecologists who believe in the power of observation over intervention.

Every essay, every guide, every seed recommendation passes through hands that know the weight of a trowel.
