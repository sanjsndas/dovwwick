Cookies & digital crumbs

How we use small, tasteful morsels to improve your visit — and why we keep it minimal.

Last revised: April 2026

Dovewick Verdure House uses cookies sparingly, only to enhance your browsing experience and remember your preferences. We never use invasive tracking or sell your data.

What are cookies?

Cookies are tiny text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your settings, recognise you on return visits, and understand how you interact with our digital garden.

Our cookie philosophy

  • Essential: necessary for basic site function (e.g., session management, security).
  • Preference: remember your language or region choices.
  • Analytics: we use privacy-friendly, minimal analytics to count visits and page views — no personal identifiers.
  • Marketing: we do not use third-party marketing or retargeting cookies.

List of cookies we use

  • dovewick_session – essential, expires after 24 hours.
  • dovewick_pref – stores your language preference, expires after 30 days.
  • dovewick_visit – simple counter, no personal data, expires after 7 days.
  • dovewick_consent – records your cookie consent choice, expires after 1 year.

Your choice

You can accept or decline non‑essential cookies when you first visit. You can also manage cookie settings in your browser at any time. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect site functionality.

Third‑party services

We embed content from trusted partners (e.g., garden maps, event calendars). These services may set their own cookies, but we only work with partners who respect privacy.

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please reach out via the garden studio details below.
