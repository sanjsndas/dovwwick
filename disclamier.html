Disclamier

Honest words about our garden, our advice, and the nature of cultivating knowledge.

Last revised: April 2026

Dovewick Verdure House is a space for inspiration, learning, and gentle curiosity. The information we share is offered in good faith, but we invite you to tend your own garden with care.

General notice

All content on this website — including articles, guides, plant profiles, and seasonal advice — is for informational and educational purposes only. While we strive for accuracy, we cannot guarantee that every detail is complete or current. Gardening is an art rooted in place, climate, and personal intuition.

No professional advice

Our writings do not replace professional horticultural, botanical, or landscape design consultation. Always seek the guidance of a qualified specialist for specific planting, soil, or pest management decisions. Dovewick does not assume liability for any outcomes resulting from the use of our content.

External links & resources

We occasionally link to trusted external sources, suppliers, and garden archives. These are provided for your convenience; we do not endorse or assume responsibility for the content, policies, or practices of third‑party sites. We encourage you to review their own disclamiers and privacy notices.

Affiliate & product mentions

We may mention tools, books, or seeds that we truly love. Some of these references may include affiliate links, which help support our work at no extra cost to you. We only recommend items we have used and admired in our own garden.

Personal responsibility

Your garden journey is yours alone. We invite you to experiment, adapt, and observe. Dovewick is a companion, not a manual. We believe in the wisdom of hands in the soil and the patience of seasons.

If you have any questions about this disclamier, please write to us — we’d love to hear from you.
